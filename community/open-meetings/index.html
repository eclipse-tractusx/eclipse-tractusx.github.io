Open Meetings

This page hosts information about all of our open Tractus-X meetings. These are dedicated sync meetings for specific products, as well as open planning sessions.

Regular meetings​

EDC Weekly | Extended

Every Tuesday from 10:30 am to 11:00 am CET

Open house meeting to support with anything EDC related. You have questions about EDC, problems running/ configuring EDC in your environment or want to know what´s next in EDC and when to expect? This is the place to ask all these questions.

  • Contact:
  • benjamin.bw.westphal@mercedes-benz.com

NewJoiner - Office Hour

Every 4 weeks on Friday effective 8. Mar 2024 until 31. Dec 2024 from 02:05 pm to 03:00 pm CET

Open hour meeting for all new joiner. The goal of the meeting is to provide an easy entry on all topics. The meeting will only take place if specific questions have been submitted. Please reach out in the first place to Stephan Bauer

One-time meetings​

Tractus-X Open Planning R24.08

Occurs on Wednesday 10. Apr 2024 and Thursday 11. Apr 2024 from 08:00 am to 12:00 am CET

Open Planning, hosted by the Network Service Committee of the Catena-X association. The goal for this meeting is planning of the intended work content for the next program increment - which contains also preparation of future release content, refactoring, general architectural work, tool & process improvements and feature planning for the next Tractus-X release. More information (also an agenda) can be found under the Additional Links.

  • stephan.bauer@catena-x.net
