Open Source Planning for Tractus-X release 24.05

December 11, 2023 · 2 min read
Stephan Bauer
Platform Domain Manager

Dear Tractus-X Community Members 🎉,

We are excited to extend this invitation to you for our upcoming Tractus-X meetings. These meetings are a great opportunity for both business stakeholders and developers to contribute and collaborate on the future of Tractus-X. Please feel free to forward this post to anyone interested in contributing, whether in planning or development.

Upcoming Meetings:

  • Tractus-X Guardrails
    • Date: 13th December 2023
    • Time: 13:00 - 14:00
    • Meetinglink
  • Tractus-X Open Refinement meeting canceled
    • Date: 19th December 2023
    • Time: 09:00 - 16:00
    • Meetinglink
  • Tractus-X Open Refinement
    • Date: 10th January 2024
    • Time: 09:00 - 16:00
    • Meetinglink
  • Tractus-X Open Planning
    • Date: 17th January 2024
    • Time: 09:30 - 16:30
    • Meetinglink

Staying Informed​

To stay updated on any changes or additional information, please subscribe to our mailing list. A great way to keep informed is through the provided Tractus-X Development Mailing List RSS Feed.

tip

Here, you can also find previous messages for reference.

Feature Creation on SIG-Release Board​

As with the last PI, you can already start creating features in the SIG-Release board. Please visit SIG-Release Board on GitHub to contribute. The only change this time is the label, which should now be Prep-PI12.

Further Information​

For more details about the planning process and what these meetings entail, please refer to our guide on Open Refinement and Planning in Open Source Communities and of course the sig-release README

Stay Connected​

Follow our news section and join our Tractus-X mailing list and be part of our Matrix Chat from Eclipse Tractus-X

For more details about Tractus-X, visit the official Eclipse Tractus-X Project Page.

We look forward to your participation and contributions in shaping the future of Tractus-X.

🚀 The Tractus-X Team
