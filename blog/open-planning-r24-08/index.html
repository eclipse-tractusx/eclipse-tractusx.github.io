Tractus-X Open Planning R24.08

February 19, 2024 · 4 min read
Stephan Bauer
Platform Domain Manager

Dear Tractus-X Community Members 🎉,

we are excited to extend this invitation to you for our upcoming Tractus-X meetings. These meetings are a great opportunity for both business stakeholders and developers to contribute and collaborate on the future of Tractus-X. Please feel free to forward this post to anyone interested in contributing, whether in planning or development.

Agenda​

  • Date: 10th April and 11th April 2024
  • Time: 08:00 - 12:00
  • Please see the Open Meetings page for downloadable ICS file and session link
  • Release Planning Board

Wednesday, 10th April 2024​

  • 08:00 - 08:30 - Open Planning Vision & Intro
  • 08:30 - 09:00 - Open Planning Vision Deep Dive Tractus-X
  • 09:00 - 11:30 - Joint Open Planning (all teams)
    • 09:00 - 09:15 - Business Domain SUS
    • 09:15 - 11:15 - Platform Domain
    • 11:15 - 11:30 - Business Domain PQR
  • 11:30 - 12:00 - Open Planning Wrap-Up / Summary Day 1
info

The detailed agenda is only filtered by labelnot by status. As a prequisite, the issues need to be in status Backlog.

Staying Informed​

To stay updated on any changes or additional information, please subscribe to our mailing list. A great way to keep informed is through the provided Tractus-X Development Mailing List RSS Feed.

tip

Further Information​

For more details about the planning process and what these meetings entail, please refer to our guide on Open Refinement and Planning in Open Source Communities and of course the sig-release README

Stay Connected​
