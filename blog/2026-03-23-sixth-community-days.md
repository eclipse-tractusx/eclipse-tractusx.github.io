Dear Eclipse Tractus-X- and Dataspace-Community,

We are thrilled to announce the **Sixth Eclipse Tractus-X Community Days**, taking place on **July 2–3, 2026 at ARENA2036 e.V. (Pfaffenwaldring 19, 70569 Stuttgart, Germany)**! 🎉

Building on the enormous success of our previous five editions, this year's Community Days once again promises to be the premier gathering for everyone passionate about **open-source data spaces, automotive innovation, and collaborative technology development**. Whether you're a developer, a solution architect, a product manager, or just curious about Tractus-X — this event has something for you.

<!-- truncate -->

### Why Attend?

The Eclipse Tractus-X Community Days are the heartbeat of our open-source ecosystem. Every edition brings together a growing, global community to share knowledge, build connections, and shape the future of the Catena-X data space together.

- 🌍 **Global Community:** Connect with contributors from across the world — including participants from Manufacturing-X initiatives such as Factory-X, Chem-X, Aerospace-X, Semiconductor-X, Construction-X, and more.
- 🛠️ **Hands-On Workshops:** Dive deep into technical sessions, practical tutorials, and real-world challenges using the latest Eclipse Tractus-X components and KITs.
- 💡 **Innovation & Use Cases:** Learn about the latest advancements, new use cases, and release highlights from the Tractus-X ecosystem.
- 🤝 **Networking:** Forge meaningful connections with fellow contributors, solution providers, and industry leaders in the Catena-X ecosystem.
- 🎤 **Keynotes & Presentations:** Hear from thought leaders, open-source pioneers, and decision-makers shaping the future of data-driven industries.
- 🧩 **Coding Challenges:** Take part in exciting coding challenges and hackathon-style sessions that let you contribute directly to the Tractus-X ecosystem.

### Looking Back — Our Community Days Journey

The Tractus-X Community Days have grown from a small inaugural gathering into a globally recognized open-source event. Here's a look back at our journey so far:

- 🥇 **[First Eclipse Tractus-X Community Days](https://arena2036.de/en/de/catena-x/tractus-x-community-days)** *(December 2023)* — The inaugural event that launched our community tradition at ARENA2036
- 🥈 **[Second Eclipse Tractus-X Community Days](https://arena2036.de/en/reader/second-eclipse-tractus-x-community-days/)** *(May 2024)* — Expanded participation, deeper technical sessions, and the first international wave of attendees
- 🥉 **[Third Eclipse Tractus-X Community Days](https://arena2036.de/en/reader/third-eclipse-tractus-x-community-days/)** *(December 2024)* — Over 220 participants from Japan, Spain, the USA, and India — a truly global event
- 4️⃣ **[Fourth Eclipse Tractus-X Community Days](https://arena2036.de/en/reader/fourth-eclipse-tractus-x-community-days/)** *(May 2025)* — International collaboration, exciting use case sharing, and growing Manufacturing-X involvement
- 5️⃣ **[Fifth Eclipse Tractus-X Community Days](https://arena2036.de/reader/fifth-eclipse-tractus-x-community-days/)** *(December 2025)* — Another milestone edition bringing the community even closer together

Each event has strengthened our community and accelerated open-source innovation in the automotive and manufacturing sectors. We're proud of how far we've come — and excited about what's next!
