### Open Refinement Session on 18.10.2023

Join us for our **Open Refinement Session**, where we'll embark on a high-level exploration of our project's features and outstanding issues. This session offers a platform for developers, stakeholders, and enthusiasts
to come together and clarify project requirements. It's an opportunity to ask questions and ensure everyone has a shared understanding of the project's direction.

By participating, you'll not only contribute to a smoother development process but also help in shaping the project's roadmap.

### Open Planning/Review Session on 26.10.2023

Join us for our **Open Planning Session**, a crucial juncture where we transition from ideation to execution. Building on the foundation laid during our refinement phase, where we meticulously detailed and polished our issues,
this session aims to chart the course for our project's future.

We'll collaboratively prioritize features, set clear milestones, and match tasks with the right talents and teams. More than just an organizational meeting, this is a space for dialogue, a chance for all contributors to weigh
in on our strategic approach.

Whether you're a core developer, a stakeholder, or someone passionate about our mission, your insights are vital to make informed decisions. Let's shape our vision into a structured, actionable plan together!
