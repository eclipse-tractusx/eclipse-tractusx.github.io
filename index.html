More Ways to Contribute

There are many ways to get involved and make a difference in the Eclipse Tractus-X ecosystem.

Create a KIT

Build and contribute your own KIT to help the community with comprehensive documentation and reference implementations.

Ask questions about KITs

Get help and support from the community by asking questions about KITs on our Matrix channel.

Join Community Discussions

Participate in our community office hours and help shape the future of dataspace technology.

Chat with Developers

Chat with us at our main Eclipse Tractus-X Matrix channel (a open source chat service), and become one of us!

Get Started

New to Eclipse Tractus-X? Follow our getting started guide to learn how to contribute.

Contribute Code

Help improve existing free and open source software (FOSS) by contributing code, fixes, and enhancements to our repositories.

Learn and Improve Documentation

Help make the content of the KITs better by fixing errors, adding examples and diagrams, or improving clarity.

Report Issues

Found a bug or issue? Report it to help us improve the quality of our KITs and reference implementations.

Share Ideas

Have an idea for a new KIT or improvement? Share it with the community and start a discussion.

Join our Community Days

Participate in our community days to learn, collaborate and share ideas with others.

Ready to Get Started?

The best way to get started is to join our community and introduce yourself. We're here to help you find the right way to contribute.
